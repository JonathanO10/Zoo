Desert Habitat is a place that doesn't get much rain and is known as the driest places on Earth. Some of the most famous deserts are the Sahara desert, Kalahari desert, Gobi desert, and many more. Some animals that live in the desert habitat are camels, coyotes, mountain lions, and many more.

Camels

Camels are most known because that have humps on its back. Poeple use camels for transportation. Some interesting facts about camels are that they could live up to 50 years, they are fast, and camels could store water in their blood.

Mountain Lions

Mountain lions are known to being friendly to humans. Mountain lions could get to as big as 8 feet long and could weight between 135-175 as ground adults. An interesting fact about the mountain lion is that they have more names than any other animal in the world
