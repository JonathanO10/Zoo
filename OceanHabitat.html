Sharks

There is around 500 different species of sharks and there could be around a billion or more sharks in the world right now. Some interesting facts about the shark is that they don't have bones and the smallest species of sharks is smaller than the human hand.

Sea turtles

Sea turtles are reptiles. There is seven species of sea turtles right now which are flatback, green, hawksbill, leatherback, loggerhead, Kemp's ridley, and olive ridley. An interesting fact about sea turtles is that they don't have teeth.
